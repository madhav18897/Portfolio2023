Thoughtcloud US Summer Internship

Product and UX/UI Design

Reno,Nevada, United States (remote)
June 2023 to August 2023

Project Context

Thoughtcloud's Vision

Thoughtcloud is a Nevada-based startup that seeks to make college students voices heard highlighting their academic professional and even personal journeys. By doing this they want to create a community of current and incoming students at universities and also help this demographic connect with student organizations on college campuses.

The Thoughtcloud Podcast

Listen to these unique educational journeys on Spotify to see how other student's and student orgs are making the impact you’ve always wanted to make.

My Role

As a Product and UX/UI Designer my responsibilities were designing information architecture, building user flows and user journeys, creating low fidelity wireframes and mockups, and high fidelity wireframes and mockups. I learned how to organize, plan, and collaborate with other team members including other designers and software developers.

What I Learned

Since this is an early stage start up, I got the opportunity to contribute to the product from the very beginning which taught me a lot about turning business ideas and insights from user research into production ready user interfaces through the entire design thinking cycle.
I also learned how to build a design system from scratch applying principles of accessibility while keeping the brand's identity as the focus. I also did this in a team environment with constant collaboration with the CEO. This help me improve my skills as an effective team member.
